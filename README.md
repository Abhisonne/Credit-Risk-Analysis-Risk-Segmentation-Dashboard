# Credit Risk Analysis & Risk Segmentation Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer financial behavior to identify key drivers of credit delinquency.  
The goal is to evaluate how liquidity, credit exposure, and repayment patterns influence default risk and support data-driven risk assessment.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key financial indicators contributing to credit risk  
- Analyze relationships between customer behavior and delinquency  
- Develop a risk segmentation approach for better classification  
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Customers with **low liquidity (checking and savings balances)** exhibit significantly higher delinquency risk  
- Higher **credit amounts and longer loan durations** are strongly associated with increased default probability  
- Financial behavior indicators have a **greater impact on risk compared to demographic attributes**  
- Customers with **high loan burden and low reserves** form the highest risk segment  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization  
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data cleaning and exploratory data analysis  
- **Excel** – Data preprocessing  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers (Age, Housing, Purpose) for dynamic filtering  
- Risk segmentation into High, Medium, and Low categories  
- Financial behavior analysis (Checking account, Saving account, Loan patterns)  
- Tooltip-enabled detailed insights for enhanced interactivity
